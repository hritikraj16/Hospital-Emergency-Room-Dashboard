# Hospital-Emergency-Room-Dashboard



## Tools Used
- Excel (Data Cleaning)
- SQL (Generating Insights)
- Power BI (Dashboard Building)

## About the Project
This dashboard analyzes hospital emergency room data from April 2023 to 
October 2024, covering 9,216 patients across 19 months.

## What the Dashboard Covers
- Monthly and consolidated view of patient visits
- Average wait time and patient satisfaction tracking
- Patient breakdown by age, gender, race, and department referral
- Busiest days and hours in the emergency room
- Individual patient details

## Key Findings
- Average wait time was around 35 minutes
- Satisfaction score was 4.99 out of 10
- Mondays and Saturdays had the highest patient volume
- Most referrals went to General Practice and Orthopedics
